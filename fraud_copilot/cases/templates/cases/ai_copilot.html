How AI Co-Pilot Works

The AI Anti-Fraud Co-Pilot operates after your existing fraud detection systems (SAS, PRM, etc.) generate alerts. It does not replace those systems.

1
Alert Received

Upstream monitoring flags a suspicious transaction and routes it to the analyst queue.

2
Risk Classification

The rule engine scores the case. High-risk alerts bypass AI automation entirely.

3
AI Analysis

For low/medium cases, the Co-Pilot summarizes indicators, explains risk, and recommends actions.

4
Analyst Decision

The employee reviews AI output and makes the final call — mark safe, escalate, or notify customer.

High-Risk Policy

High-risk cases are never handled automatically. They remain assigned to the employee or Investigation team. AI analysis and customer notification features are disabled for these alerts.
