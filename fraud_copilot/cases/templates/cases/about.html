AI Anti-Fraud Co-Pilot

This MVP demonstrates how an AI Co-Pilot can support fraud monitoring employees after initial fraud detection systems generate alerts.

It focuses on low and medium risk suspicious cases, while high-risk cases remain under manual employee or Investigation team review.

Key Principles

  • Does not replace existing fraud detection systems (SAS, PRM, etc.)
  • Operates after alerts are generated by upstream monitoring
  • High-risk cases are never handled automatically by AI
  • Final decision always remains with the human analyst
  • Reduces workload on gray-area and lower-risk suspicious cases

Important Disclaimers

Synthetic Data Only

All customer and transaction data is fabricated for demonstration.

No Real Customer Data

No real banking or personal information is used or stored.

No Real Notifications

Customer messages are simulated in the UI only.

Hackathon Demo

Built for hackathon demonstration and proof of concept.
